MVP (Minimum Viable Product also know as JUST passing) will be the following:
1. The ability to create reimbursement requests in Postman and have them persist in your database
2. The ability to pull reimbursement requests from the database
3. The ability to approve/deny request from the database and have that approval/denial persist in the database
4. The ability to create users and have them persist in the database
5. The ability to log into your application and have it query the database and have allow or disallow that log in




This pretty much means that you need to have Javalin and JDBC working
